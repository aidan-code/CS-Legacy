Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

When will the new LXP platform Launch?

What's the maximum number of users on the platform once the new LXP (Phase 1) is launched?

The Phase 1 LXP Platform will be limited to 1XXX users. The exact number will be revealed soon. When the launch campaign week is over (7 days after the launch date), new users will have to pay the RRP of £700 for 3 months' access.

What is the maximum number of times I can sign up for the Waiting List?

You can only sign up once. We've implemented a technology to track our IP address to prevent people from signing up more than once. Please be considerate and give others the opportunity to succeed.

Can I move up the waitlist and be guaranteed a spot? What other advantages do I have?

You can always move up the waiting list in the rankings, even if you register later. The more people you invite to the waitlist, the higher your rank will rise.

    The first three places will also receive special offers.

  • 1st Place: 3 Months of Access for Free
  • 2nd Place: Get a 50% discount off the launch day price (£50).
  • 3rd Place: a 30% discount off the launch day price (£70).

The next 100 places will get a 20% discount off the launch day price (£80). The remaining space left will only have to pay £100.

Why a waiting list?

Unfortunately, we see in most projects that the focus is to build up members on a Google form and get them to fulfill multiple tasks without being updated and getting insights on their contributions. For ConnectinSkillz, the waitlist allows us to make an informed decision about all our prospects and candidates while providing all the necessary information directly to you, while you wait and prepare for what is to come. We know the first few hours and days are important to you, so we focus on a quality waitlist where everyone is given an opportunity to succeed.

What is the fastest way to get the most up-to-date information?

You will get the fastest and most exclusive information when you are registered on the waitlist. We will also provide all the necessary information on the website, on Twitter and on Instagram to keep you up-to-date.
